1. Active Domain Directory Services (ADDS)
1.1. Installing Active Domain Directory Services

We have a DNS server. We now want to install a Domain Controller (DC).

Before we can do this, we first need to install Active Domain Directory Services.

To do this, proceed as follows:

Go to "Server Manager Control".

Click on "Add roles and features".

Click on "Next".

Click on "Next".

Click on "Next".

Left click on the square next to "Active Domain Directory Services" Then click on "Next >".

Click on "Add Features".

Click on "Next".

You will receive a message that you still need to install features, click on the button.

Click on "Next".

Click on "Next".

Left click on the square next to "Restart the destination server automatically if required" Click on "Yes" for an automatic restart of the server Finally click on "Install" (The server will be occupied for a few moments to process the installation.)

Click "Close" if the installation is successful.

Go to "Server Manager Control". We see that AD DS is there.

In "Server Manager Control", Click with the left mouse button on the yellow triangle next to the flag. Then click on "Promote the server to a domain controller".

Click on "Next".

Enter your password and click on "Next".

Uncheck and click on "Next".

Click on “Next”.

Click on “Next”.

Click on “Next”.

Click on “Next”.

The server has rebooted!!!

1.2. Creating a user account in Active Directory on the domain controller

Double click on "Active Directory Users and Computers".

Right click on the "Users" folder and select "New" -> "User".

Enter your "First name"

Enter your "Last name"

Enter your "Use Logon name"

Click on "Next"

Enter your Password.

Uncheck "User must change password at next logon".

And click on "Next".

1.4. Properties of a domain account

Select the "Account" tab and click on "Logon Hours..."

Blue are the times at which Janssens can log in. Make sure to get the same logon times as shown above. Click "OK" when ready, then click the "Logon To..." button.

Here you can set which computers Jansens can log in to, by default he can log in to all computers, set that he can only log in to "PC1". Click "OK". Close Jansens dialog boxes and test whether he can log in to your PC.

Janssens is on sick leave for 3 months, you must ensure that no one logs in under his name during his absence. Click on the "Account" tab in the properties of Janssens and activate the checkbox "Account is disabled". Close Jansens dialog boxes and test whether he can log in to your PC.
