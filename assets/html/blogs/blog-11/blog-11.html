Windows Server 2019 Organizational Unit OU Setup



1. Organizational Unit Introduction

An Organizational Unit (OU) is a subdivision within Active Directory into which you can place users, groups, computers, and other Organizational Units. You can create Organizational Unit to reflect the functional or corporate structure of your organization.

2. Organizational Unit Introduction

Right click on your domain and select "New" --> "Organizational unit"

Click "OK" to create the OU "TEST".
